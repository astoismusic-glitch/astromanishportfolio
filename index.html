About Artist

Hindi poetry, dark melody, trap pressure.

AstroManish is a Hindi poetry based musician with knowledge across genres and a self-made indie approach: music production, mix and master, rap, writing, distribution, and creative direction. His sound is for listeners who connect with dark melodies, dark lyrics, heavy drums, smooth flows, and emotional honesty.

Based in Mohali, originally from Sultanpur, Uttar Pradesh, and born in Ludhiana, Punjab in 2004, AstroManish brings a strong creative perspective at a young age. His upcoming mixtape carries three years of work, learning, and lived emotion.
